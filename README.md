## 👋 About

I'm a software engineer at **Nesia Inc.** and a researcher working at the intersection of **computer vision, retrieval-augmented generation, and agent-guided systems**. I recently completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at the **Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Dhaka**, where I co-authored conference papers in robotics, low-light image restoration, and medical image analysis.

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Computer Vision** - image restoration, segmentation, object detection, and applied medical imaging
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** - grounded LLM systems for high-stakes domains
- **Agent-guided Systems** - LLM-driven planning and orchestration for real-world workflows
- **Adjacent interests** - Quantum Computing · Functional Programming · Distributed Systems

## 📚 Publications

- **A Hybrid Residual U-Net Architecture for Enhanced Low-Light Image Restoration** - *Conference paper, July 2025.* Deep learning architecture (hybrid residual U-Net) for image restoration in poor-lighting conditions.
- **Design and Development of an RC Ornithopter for Remote Surveillance** - *Conference paper, July 2025.* Bio-inspired flapping-wing UAV for agile low-speed surveillance flight.
- **An Automated Framework for Segmenting and Identifying Thyroid Nodules in Ultrasound Images** - *Conference paper, December 2024.* Computer-vision pipeline for computer-aided diagnosis of thyroid cancer from ultrasound.
